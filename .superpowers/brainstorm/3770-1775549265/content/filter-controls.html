A — Inline Chip Toggles

Risk levels shown as always-visible toggle chips above the list. Click to activate/deactivate. Sort and Region as compact dropdowns on the right. Fast, always visible, no hover needed.

C — Persistent Filter Sidebar

Dedicated left panel always visible alongside the list. All filters in one place, always reachable. Takes horizontal space but nothing is hidden. Power-user feel, like Bloomberg / data terminals.
