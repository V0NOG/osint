A — Outline Only

Thin semi-transparent border lines drawn along country edges. Globe surface stays dark. Risk dot markers remain as the primary risk signal. Clean, minimal — like a classified ops map. Easiest to implement (lines only, no fill geometry).

B — Filled by Risk Level

Country polygons filled with a subtle tint matching their risk color (red for critical, orange for high, etc.) plus a matching border. The globe becomes a live risk heatmap. Critical/high countries still get pulsing dot markers on top. Immediately readable — you see the risk picture at a glance.
